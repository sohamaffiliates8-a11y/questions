Soham sci ch16– Q&A + Summary

Q1
What is heredity?
Heredity is the passing of traits from parents to their children through genes.
Summary (English):
Traits pass from parents to kids through genes.
Summary (Hinglish):
Parents ke traits genes ke through children tak jaate hain.
Q2
What is variation?
Variation means the differences seen among individuals of the same species.
Summary (English):
Differences among individuals of same species.
Summary (Hinglish):
Ek hi species ke logon mein jo differences hote hain unhe variation kehte hain.
Q3
Define gene.
A gene is a unit of DNA that controls the expression of a specific trait.
Summary (English):
Gene controls a specific trait.
Summary (Hinglish):
Gene DNA ka part hota hai jo kisi trait ko control karta hai.
Q4
What are chromosomes?
Chromosomes are thread-like structures in the nucleus that carry genes.
Summary (English):
Chromosomes carry genes inside the nucleus.
Summary (Hinglish):
Chromosomes nucleus mein hote hain aur genes ko carry karte hain.
Q5
What is DNA?
DNA is the molecule that stores genetic information.
Summary (English):
DNA stores genetic info.
Summary (Hinglish):
DNA genetic information ko store karta hai.
Q6
What do we mean by inherited traits?
Traits that pass from parents to offspring through genes.
Summary (English):
Inherited traits come from parents.
Summary (Hinglish):
Parents se jo traits bachchon tak jaate hain.
Q7
What are acquired traits?
Traits gained during life due to environment or habits, not passed genetically.
Summary (English):
Traits learned or developed, not inherited.
Summary (Hinglish):
Life ke experience se aaye traits, genes se nahi.
Q8
What is a dominant trait?
A trait that appears even if only one dominant gene is present.
Summary (English):
Dominant trait shows even with one gene.
Summary (Hinglish):
Ek dominant gene bhi ho toh trait dikh jaata hai.
Q9
What is a recessive trait?
A trait that appears only if both genes are recessive.
Summary (English):
Recessive needs two recessive genes.
Summary (Hinglish):
Recessive trait tabhi dikhta hai jab dono genes recessive ho.
Q10
Define heredity and variation.
Heredity is passing of traits. Variation is differences among individuals.
Summary (English):
Heredity = passing traits, Variation = differences.
Summary (Hinglish):
Heredity traits pass hoti hain, variation differences hoti hain.
Q11
Who is known as the father of genetics?
Gregor Mendel is known as the father of genetics.
Summary (English):
Mendel founded genetics.
Summary (Hinglish):
Genetics ke father Mendel hain.
Q12
What experiment did Mendel perform?
He performed hybridization experiments on pea plants.
Summary (English):
Mendel studied pea plant traits.
Summary (Hinglish):
Mendel ne pea plants par experiments kiye.
Q13
What are Mendel’s laws?
Law of Dominance, Law of Segregation, and Law of Independent Assortment.
Summary (English):
Mendel gave 3 major laws of inheritance.
Summary (Hinglish):
Mendel ne 3 laws diye jo inheritance samjhate hain.
Q14
What is a trait?
A characteristic like height, eye color, etc., determined by genes.
Summary (English):
Trait = any characteristic.
Summary (Hinglish):
Trait kisi bhi characteristic ko kehte hain.
Q15
What is the role of environment in traits?
Environment affects acquired traits and sometimes influences inherited traits.
Summary (English):
Environment can modify traits.
Summary (Hinglish):
Environment traits ko change ya influence kar sakta hai.
Q16
What is genetic material?
DNA is the primary genetic material in most organisms.
Summary (English):
Genetic material = DNA.
Summary (Hinglish):
Genetic material DNA hota hai.
Q17
What is a DNA fingerprint?
A technique to identify individuals using DNA patterns.
Summary (English):
DNA pattern identification.
Summary (Hinglish):
DNA patterns se person ko identify karna.
Q18
What are alleles?
Different forms of the same gene.
Summary (English):
Alleles = gene variants.
Summary (Hinglish):
Alleles ek hi gene ke alag versions hote hain.
Q19
What is a Punnett square?
A tool used to predict genetic combinations.
Summary (English):
Punnett square predicts offspring traits.
Summary (Hinglish):
Punnett square se traits ka prediction hota hai.
Q20
What is mutation?
A sudden change in DNA sequence that may create variation.
Summary (English):
Mutation = sudden DNA change.
Summary (Hinglish):
Mutation DNA ya RNA ke code mein hone wali galti ya change hai. DNA hamare body ka "blueprint" hai jisme saari instructions (nirdesh) hoti hain. Jab yeh code badalta hai, toh usse banne wale proteins bhi alag ho sakte hain, jisse body ke functions par asar pad sakta hai
